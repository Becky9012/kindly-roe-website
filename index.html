Instructions

To test your transparent logo:

  1. Place your logo file in this directory and name it logo.png
  2. Refresh this page to see how your logo looks on different backgrounds
  3. Check that the logo is clearly visible and maintains good contrast on all backgrounds
  4. Test different sizes to ensure the logo scales well
